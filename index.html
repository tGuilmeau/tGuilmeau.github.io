Welcome on my webpage!

I am a PhD student (since October 2021) in the INRIA Saclay team OPIS, working under the supervision of Emilie Chouzenoux and Víctor Elvira.

My main research focus is the study of Monte Carlo methods in statistics and global optimization through non-Euclidean optimization schemes and information geometry. I am also interested in the study of biological models such as the chemostat.
