Welcome on my webpage!

Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at Statify, a joint team of INRIA Grenoble Alpes and Laboratoire Jean Kuntzmann.

In January 2025, I completed my PhD in the INRIA Saclay team OPIS under the supervision of Emilie Chouzenoux and VÃ­ctor Elvira.

I have been working on the following topics:
  • Stochastic optimization algorithms with a non-Euclidean twist (mirror descent, Bregman-based methods, natural gradients) as well as generalized/abstract convex analysis,
  • Theoretical foundations of methods used for Bayesian statistics (adaptive importance sampling, variational inference) and global optimization (evolution strategies),
  • Optimal control and viability of dynamical systems, focusing on biological processes such as the chemostat.
