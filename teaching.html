Outreach

In 2024, I helped animate a research workshop for high school students during the RJMI 2024.

In 2023, I helped animate a workshop at the 2023 Paris-Saclay science fair.

During the year 2022, I carried out a scientific outreach mission funded by INRIA Saclay. I had the opportunity to:
  • Present my research topics and the ins and outs of research to high school students during the Maths Week,
  • Guide a group of high school girls through an open research problem for two days during the RJMI 2022,
  • Animate workshops at the 2022 Paris-Saclay science fair, including unplugged programming activities,
  • Coordinate the filming of interviews of OPIS researchers by students from the BUT MMI. I also helped the students with the scientific background they needed to prepare their questions.
